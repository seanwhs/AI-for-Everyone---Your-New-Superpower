## üß≠ The Golden Rule of Prompting: "P.R.T.C."

When you talk to an AI (like Gemini), don't just give it a one-word command. Use the **P.R.T.C.** method to get the best results:

1. **P**ersona: Tell the AI who it is (e.g., "Act as a professional chef").
2. **R**equest: Tell it exactly what to do (e.g., "Write a recipe for dinner").
3. **T**arget: Who is this for? (e.g., "For a family with 2 toddlers").
4. **C**onstraints: Any rules? (e.g., "Must be under 30 minutes and no nuts").

## üß™ Lab Cheat Sheet: Sarcopenia Project

Follow these steps during our afternoon lab session:

### Step 1: The Research Phase (Gemini)

Copy and paste this prompt into [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com):

> "Act as a Senior Health Consultant. Write a 500-word article titled 'Muscle Strength for Seniors: Beating Sarcopenia.' Include a list of 5 safe, equipment-free exercises like chair squats or wall push-ups. Use a friendly, encouraging tone."

### Step 2: The Deep Dive (NotebookLM)

1. Save your Gemini article as a **PDF** or copy the text into a **Google Doc**.
2. Go to [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google/).
3. Create a New Notebook and upload your file.
4. Click **Notebook Guide** > **Generate Audio Overview**.
5. Put on your headphones and listen to your AI-created "Podcast"!
