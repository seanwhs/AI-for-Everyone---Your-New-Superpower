## üÜò Troubleshooting "Weird" AI Answers
1.  **If it's too long:** Say "Give me the short version."
2.  **If it's too complicated:** Say "Explain it like I'm five."
3.  **If it's wrong:** Say "That is incorrect because [X]. Try again."
4.  **The Golden Rule:** Always verify important facts with a Google search or a human expert!
