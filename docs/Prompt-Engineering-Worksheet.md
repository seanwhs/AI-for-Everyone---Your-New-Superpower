### **Solution for challenge 4**

For the word **"DISCONNECTED"**, the AI typically breaks it into these three tokens:

1. `[DIS]` (The Prefix)
2. `[CONNECT]` (The Root)
3. `[ED]` (The Suffix)

### **Why this matters**

This "Lego brick" approach is the secret to AIâ€™s intelligence. Instead of memorizing millions of individual words, the AI learns the **logic** of the pieces:

* **Grammar Logic:** By isolating `[ED]`, the AI knows that any word ending in this token likely refers to the past. It doesn't need to be taught "walked," "jumped," and "connected" separately; it just learns the rule of the `[ED]` brick.
* **Meaning Logic:** By isolating `[DIS]`, the AI understands "negation" or "reversal." If it knows what "connect" means, the `[DIS]` brick tells it that the meaning is now the opposite.
* **Efficiency:** This allows the AI to understand words it has **never seen before**. If a new word is invented tomorrow, the AI will break it into known tokens to "guess" the meaning with high accuracy.

### ## Part 3: The Prompt Refiner (Troubleshooting)

When the first answer isn't perfect, **iterate.** Use this guide to diagnose and fix the issue.

| Problem | Diagnosis | The Fix (Follow-up Prompt) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Too wordy** | Missing Constraints | "Simplify this into 3 bullet points, under 15 words each." |
| **Cold/Formal tone** | Weak Persona | "Change Persona to a supportive mentor. Make it warmer." |
| **Making things up** | Loose Context | "Use ONLY the uploaded text. If not found, say 'I don't know'." |
| **Too generic** | Basic Persona | "Act as an Expert Data Analyst. Critique this for gaps." |
| **Wall of text** | Missing Template | "Convert this list into a side-by-side comparison table." |
