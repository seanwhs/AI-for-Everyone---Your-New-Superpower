## 2. The "Certificate of Completion" Checklist
Before a trainer directs a student to the `final-quiz.html`, ensure the following are met:

- [ ] **Lab 1 Complete:** Student has generated a personalized Sarcopenia exercise plan in Gemini.
- [ ] **Lab 2 Complete:** Student has successfully "grounded" a source in NotebookLM and generated an Audio Overview.
- [ ] **Privacy Awareness:** Student can explain why we don't put sensitive personal data into the "Open Web" version of Gemini.
- [ ] **The "Final Quiz":** Student has achieved a 100% score on the `final-quiz.html` (2 out of 2).
