**Title:** "Investing in AI Literacy: The Business Case for Training"

Artificial Intelligence is reshaping workplace dynamics, and employees equipped with AI skills can enhance efficiency, creativity, and decision-making. The “AI for Everyone” workshop provides organizations with a structured, hands-on approach to AI literacy.

The program combines foundational knowledge, practical tool use, and ethical guidance. Employees learn to use AI tools like Google Gemini and NotebookLM, apply structured prompting (P.R.T.C.), and differentiate between creative and factual outputs. Post-course reflections and knowledge checks ensure participants internalize the skills and can apply them effectively in business contexts.

This training helps organizations foster a culture of innovation, reduce repetitive workload, and prepare teams for AI-augmented workflows. By investing in AI literacy, businesses position themselves to adapt quickly to technological change while promoting responsible and efficient AI usage.

**Organizational Benefits:**

* Upskill employees in AI literacy and tool proficiency.
* Encourage efficient workflows and creative problem-solving.
* Promote ethical and responsible AI integration.
* Provide measurable outcomes through post-course assessments.
