## 4. Lab-Specific Secrets

### Module: The P.R.T.C. Drill
Before Lab 1, do a live demo. Ask the class for a bad prompt.
* **Bad:** "Give me a workout."
* **The Fix:** Work with the class to add **P**ersona (Physio), **R**equest (3-day plan), **T**arget (Seniors), and **C**onstraints (No equipment). 
* *Observation:* Watch for the "Aha!" moment when the AI response goes from generic to genius.

### Lab 1: Sarcopenia & Gemini
* **The Pivot:** If students finish early, challenge them: "Tell the AI you have a sore left knee. See how it adjusts the exercises."

### Lab 2: NotebookLM & Audio
* **The Environment:** This part gets loud. Encourage students to step into the hallway or use headphones to hear their "AI Podcast."
* **Grounding:** Emphasize that NotebookLM *only* knows what they uploaded. It won't "hallucinate" external facts as much as Gemini.
