# Lab Guide: The Sarcopenia Project (labs/lab1-sarcopenia.md)

## Objective
Create a multimedia health guide for seniors using two different AI tools.

## Step 1: Research (Gemini)
1. Open [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com).
2. Use the "Sarcopenia Prompt" found in the `guides/learner-guide.md`.
3. Refine the article until it includes a 5-step exercise routine.
4. Copy the final text into a Google Doc and download it as a **PDF**.

## Step 2: The Librarian (NotebookLM)
1. Open [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google/).
2. Create a new notebook titled "Seniors Fitness."
3. Upload your Sarcopenia PDF.
4. Ask: "Summarize the top 3 safety tips for these exercises."

## Step 3: Multimedia Output
1. Click on **Notebook Guide** (bottom right).
2. Click **Generate** under "Deep Dive Audio Overview."
3. Listen to the AI "hosts" discuss your article!
