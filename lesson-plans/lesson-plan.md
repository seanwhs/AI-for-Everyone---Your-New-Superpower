## Section 2: The Architecture of Intelligence

* **Analogy:** Imagine your phoneâ€™s autocompleteâ€”but on steroids. AI predicts the next token (word/piece) in a sequence using probability, not memory.
* **Implication:** It can mimic reasoning, coding, and creativityâ€”but it doesnâ€™t â€œunderstandâ€ or have consciousness.
