## **Module 3: The Art of Prompting**

* **Slide 6: How to Talk to an AI**
* Formula: **Role + Task + Constraints = Success.**
* Example: "Act as a [Chef] + Write a [Recipe] + Use only [Ingredients in my fridge]."


* **Slide 7: The "Golden Rule"**
* "Trust, but Verify." AI can make mistakes (Hallucinations). Always check the important facts!
