## üß† Quick ML/AI Tips

* **AI ‚â† Magic:** It predicts what comes next based on **patterns**.
* **LLMs ‚Äúread‚Äù billions of texts** ‚Üí good at language, summarization, and reasoning.
* **Tokenization:** AI sees text in **small chunks (tokens)** like Lego bricks.
* **ML Types:**

  * **Supervised:** Learns from labeled data (e.g., kNN).
  * **Unsupervised:** Finds patterns in unlabeled data (e.g., k-Means).
  * **Reinforcement:** Learns by trial & error, optimizing rewards.
* **Overfitting:** Learns noise ‚Üí fails on new data.
* **Underfitting:** Fails to capture patterns ‚Üí poor predictions.
