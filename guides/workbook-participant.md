## **Part 4: Ethics, Bias, & The "Human Filter"**

AI is trained on human dataâ€”which means it inherits human **biases**. It reflects the data it was fed, which is often skewed toward specific demographics or viewpoints.

[Image showing AI bias and data representation in machine learning]

### **The "3-Step Verification" Model**

Before adopting an AI's suggestion, run it through this filter:

1. **The Source Check:** If I asked a human expert this, would they agree?
2. **The Bias Check:** Is this advice assuming everyone has the same amount of money, time, or physical ability?
3. **The "Socratic" Check:** Ask the AI: *"List three reasons why the advice you just gave me might be wrong or biased."*
